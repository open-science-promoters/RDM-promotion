Your experience will help us promoting research data management.
and vice versa !

The primary aim of this 'open sciency' project is to create short videos everyone can use to raise the scientists' awareness on RDM issues. In order to design and create amazing material, we gather (your?) favorite stories - personal or not - about data management and open data. On top of being interesting per se, the provided data will allow us to choose the most representative topics.

  • Be part of the project, share your expertise with the world!

  • Material excavated from the web for re-use

  • Browse and comment on stories and ideas (collected in a blog format)

  • Material created inside this project
